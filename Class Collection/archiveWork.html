Project 1: Interactive Audio & Visual with p5.js

Final: p5.js editor link

This project is an interactive multimedia design, inspired by the panel of the DJ turn table. Users can change audio parameters by moving the red circle on the control panel. Two variable will be changed based on the (x,y) of the circle in the grey square area, low EQ and resonance. Changing of these two audio parameters will lead to the change of size and density of the halftone effect in the upper right area.

Concept (Google Doc)
Final: GitHub live site

"The Life of an Eraser" is a browser-based hypernarrative that follows a small pink eraser through different pages of a digital notebook. Each page shifts color, layout, and text tone as the eraser moves, echoing the feeling of rewriting, fixing, and sometimes over-erasing moments in our lives. Instead of a single linear storyline, the project invites viewers to click around, get a little lost, and discover fragments of memory and interface glitches. I wanted the piece to feel like a quiet loop between perfectionism and letting go, the way we constantly edit ourselves online.
